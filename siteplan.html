Site Purpose

Many travellers going to Africa, may find it difficult to navigate the airlines, hotels or car rental simpley because Africa is not so much present on the grid. The Website is here to provide real time information for:

  1. Airline routes, seat, destination cities and cheaper tickets.
  2. Available hotels in the destination citiy.
  3. Car rental and game drive options.
  4. Best offers of game watch and visits.

Scenarios:

I want to visit Uganda, but i have no information of what flights are going there, or what hotels are good and near the game parks.

I need cheap tickets to Uganda or Africa, where do i get them. Most airlines fly to Europe and American cities.

What should i expect to see while in Africa or Uganda.
